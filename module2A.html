Fujitsu on Campus

Fujitsu on Campus Fujitsu will be coming to campus on 24th February to host an exclusive talk for KU students. Don’t miss this opportunity to hear from one of Fujitsu’s distinguished Cyber Security Engineers. The session will include a presentation on forensic analysis of a memory dump, and some network traffic. The technical demonstration will focus on the gathering of artefacts associated with malware infections, collected from an infected host, and how Fujitsu identify the infection process and any network traffic from the host. Fujitsu will highlight some key tools and resources used to enable these investigations and provide a real world example of identifying and tracking a banking Trojan affecting UK PLC. A Q&A will follow the talk. 2pm Wednesday 24th Feb 2016, JG1004 Register Online at https://www.kingston.ac.uk/careers/events/registration.php?event=4OQY4ZTEF0OEDFOMOKLK
